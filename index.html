FAQ

How Tembusu keeps your confidentiality

If your question isn't answered here, reach out directly — we're happy to chat.

What information do you collect?
None. We only collect what's needed to assess the lead — the customer's contact and property details. We do not need your name, email, or phone number.
How do I track the progress of my referral?
When you submit a referral, you'll receive a unique 8-character tracking code (e.g. ABCD-EFGH). Use this code on the Tracking page to check the status of your lead and view any messages from our loans team. Save it somewhere secure — this code is your only link to the referral.
What if I lose my tracking code?
Since we don't collect any identifying information from you, we can't look up your code on our end. If you lose it, visit the Recovery page and describe the referral (approximate submission date, customer's last four digits, etc.). An admin will manually match it and send you a replacement code via a channel you nominate. This is the only scenario where you'd need to identify yourself to us.
How much do I earn per referral?
You earn 50% of the processing fee on every successfully disbursed loan. For example, on a $1,000,000 loan, your referral commission could be $5,000–$10,000 depending on the deal. Commission is triggered once the customer signs and the loan disburses — not on submissions or approvals alone.
How do I receive my commission?
When the referral reaches "payout ready" status, you'll see a "Provide payout details" option on your tracking page. At that point, simply choose your mode of payment for the transfer.
How quickly will the customer receive a response?
For straightforward property-backed cases, we typically respond within one business day. Complex deal structures may take slightly longer.
Is there a minimum deal size?
No strict minimum, but our property-backed loans typically range from $100K upwards. If you're unsure whether a case qualifies, submit it — we'd rather assess and decline quickly than have you second-guess a viable deal.
What types of loans can I refer?
We work with referral partners on business loans, mortgage loans, and personal loans — all secured against private property (landed, condo, or commercial).
Does Tembusu contact the customer directly?
Only after you submit the referral. Our loans team reaches out to the customer to progress the assessment. Your involvement is only disclosed at your discretion.
Do you use cookies, analytics, or third-party tracking?
No. No Google Analytics, no tracking pixels or CDNs. All assets are served from our own domain.
Is Tembusu a licensed lender?
Yes. Tembusu Financial Services is a direct private lender operating from Singapore. Our subsidiary TBS Union Pte Ltd holds a moneylender licence regulated by the Ministry of Law.
